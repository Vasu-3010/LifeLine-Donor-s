About Us

Lifeline Donor's is a digital platform that acts as a crucial bridge between blood donors and recipients. Our mission is to ensure that every person in need receives safe and compatible blood on time.

Our services include blood donation registration, finding the nearest blood banks, and providing information about blood compatibility. Lifeline Donor's believes in building a community where every individual can contribute to saving lives.

Our team is constantly working towards creating an efficient and trustworthy system by utilizing modern technology. Every drop matters, and your one step can save someone's life.
